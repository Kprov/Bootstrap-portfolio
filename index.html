About

Growing up in Evanston, IL, Chicago has been home nearly all of my life. After finishing undergrad at Columbia College Chicago with a degree in Arts Management, I began my career in the Chicago tech community at Reverb.com. There, I grew from boxing and packaging inventory in a part time position to managing two experimental subsidiaries, managing a team of 5, domestic and international, and opened Reverb's first international office in Amsterdam of the course over four months in the summer of 2017.

I'm now beginning a new journey, with the goal of reinventing my professional portfolio with a background in both business and full-stack development. I'm incredibly excited to see where my new path leads and to continue deploying new projects regularly!
